Additional Resources

This appendix provides a curated list of additional resources to further your learning and exploration in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. These resources include official documentation, recommended courses, influential research papers, vibrant community platforms, and relevant GitHub repositories.

Recommended Courses​

  • Deep Learning Specialization (Coursera - Andrew Ng): Foundational for understanding the AI concepts behind robotics.
  • Robotics Specialization (Coursera - University of Pennsylvania): Covers robot kinematics, dynamics, and control.
  • Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control (Northwestern University): A comprehensive textbook and online course.
  • ROS 2 Basics in 5 Days (Robotics & AI Academy): Practical, hands-on introduction to ROS 2.
  • NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute (DLI): Offers various courses on GPU computing, AI, and robotics, often with Isaac Sim content.

Research Papers​

Stay updated with cutting-edge research by exploring these influential papers and fields:

  • Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robotics: Explore papers by Google Brain, OpenAI, DeepMind.
  • Sim-to-Real Transfer: Look for research on domain randomization, policy transfer, and system identification.
  • Human-Robot Interaction: Search for papers on multi-modal interfaces, social navigation, and safety.
  • Foundation Models in Robotics: Emerging research on how large pre-trained models (including LLMs) are being adapted for robotic control and perception.

Community Resources​

Engage with the robotics community to ask questions, share knowledge, and collaborate.

  • ROS Discourse Forum: discourse.ros.org
  • Robotics Stack Exchange: robotics.stackexchange.com
  • NVIDIA Omniverse Discord: For Isaac Sim and Omniverse related discussions.
  • OpenAI Community Forum: For discussions on GPT models and Whisper.
  • GitHub: Explore trending repositories in robotics and AI.

GitHub Repositories​

Many open-source projects and code examples are available on GitHub.

  • ros2/examples: Official ROS 2 example packages.
  • ros-planning/navigation2: The Nav2 stack repository.
  • IntelRealSense/realsense-ros: ROS wrapper for RealSense cameras.
  • NVIDIA-AI-IOT/isaac_ros_visual_slam: Isaac ROS Visual SLAM package.
  • openai/whisper: OpenAI's official Whisper repository.
  • bulletphysics/bullet3: The Bullet Physics engine.
  • leggedrobotics/legged_gym: Example RL environments in Isaac Gym.
  • humanoid-robotics/modern_robotics: Code examples accompanying the Modern Robotics textbook.
